Residential Waterproofing Solutions

The best line of defense against below grade moisture intrusion.

  1. Reduces heat loss, improves energy efficiency
  2. Prevents air leakage and soil gas entry
  3. Prohibits moisture from the outside air, from the ground, from the inside air, as well as that introduced during construction from getting into the basement
  4. Channels rain water and ground water run-off away from the home

Over the past 20 years, our applicators have waterproofed millions of square feet of exterior wall space, enabling building owners and builders to maximize available living space at an affordable cost.


GMX waterproofing systems are compatible with a wide range of insulation, drainage or protection course materials that provide insulating value, energy savings in addition to moisture intrusion protection.
